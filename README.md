### 🔐 Task 5 – Cybercrime Awareness Resource

#### Case Study: UPI / Online Payment Fraud

**How Fraud Works:**

1. Target selection
2. Building trust
3. Creating a pretext
4. Sending collect request
5. Victim enters PIN
6. Money is transferred

**Who is at Risk:**

* Students
* First-time users
* Online marketplace users (OLX, etc.)
* Elderly individuals

**Consequences:**

* Financial loss
* Psychological stress
* Limited legal recovery

#### 🛡️ Prevention Checklist

* Use strong, unique passwords (12+ characters)
* Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)
* Never share OTPs or PINs
* UPI Rule: Entering PIN = sending money
* Avoid public Wi-Fi for banking
* Review app permissions regularly

**Report Cybercrime:**
Website: https://cybercrime.gov.in
Helpline: 1930
